Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Digital Products

Because our products are digital downloads, all sales are generally final once the file has been delivered. We are unable to offer refunds on digital products once the download has been accessed, as they cannot be returned.

Technical Issues

If you experience a technical issue accessing your purchase — such as a broken download link or file that won't open — contact us immediately and we will help resolve it. We want every customer to be able to access what they paid for.

Refund Requests

If you believe a refund is warranted under exceptional circumstances, contact us and we will review your request on a case-by-case basis. We handle each situation with fairness and discretion.

Membership Subscriptions

Monthly and annual membership subscriptions can be cancelled at any time through your Gumroad account or by contacting us. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. We do not offer prorated refunds for partial months.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or report a technical issue, contact us at:
support@architectureofthemind.com

Please include your order number and a description of the issue. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
